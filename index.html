Femicides in Mexico

948 out of every 100,000 women are killed each year. Their deaths are the results of gender based killings.

But, what is a femicide?

A femicide is fairly recent term used to describe or reference the killing of women or girls due to their sex.

Most of the killings committed that fall under the category of femicides are committed by men.
